What is Mental Health?

According to the World Health Organization (WHO), mental health includes "subjective well-being, perceived self-efficacy, autonomy, competence, inter-generational dependence, and self-actualization of one's intellectual and emotional potential, among others."

How can I look after my Mental Health?

There are many ways to look after your mental health, and no one way is the right way. It is very individual. While it is important to seek medical help and talok to your Doctor if you think you might be unwell, Self Care is something you can do on your own or with friends to improve your overall life
